B: Good afternoon, Mr. Carter. Let me pull up your file. Yes, I see you're here for a general check-up. Have you filled in the health questionnaire we sent you?
A: Yes, I completed it online yesterday.
B: Perfect. The doctor will be with you in about ten minutes. While you wait, I need to update a few details. Has your address changed since your last visit?
A: Yes, I moved to 14 Maple Street last month.
B: And your postcode?
A: It's SW4 7BN.
B: Thank you. Are you still taking the same medication — the blood pressure tablets?
A: Yes, but my GP increased the dosage last week to ten milligrams.
B: I'll make a note of that. Do you have any new allergies or medical conditions to report?
A: No, nothing new.
B: Great. The doctor may want to do a blood test today. Have you eaten anything in the last four hours?
A: I had a light breakfast at eight this morning, so that's about seven hours ago.
B: That should be fine. Please take a seat and the nurse will call you shortly.
