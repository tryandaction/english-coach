A: Hello, I'm calling about the community volunteer fair this Saturday. I'd like to register as a visitor.
B: Of course. Registration is free, but we ask people to book a time slot because the hall can get crowded.
A: What time does it open?
B: The doors open at nine-thirty, and the first presentation starts at ten. Most visitors choose the ten-to-twelve slot.
A: I'll take that one. Is the fair still at Riverside Hall?
B: Yes, Riverside Hall on King Street. If you're driving, use the north car park because the south one is reserved for staff.
A: Thanks. Will there be information about environmental projects?
B: Yes, that area is in the blue zone near the main stage. Health and education charities are in the green zone.
A: Great. Do I need to print my booking?
B: No, just bring the confirmation email on your phone.
