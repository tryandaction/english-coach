A: Hello, I'd like to enroll in the evening language courses. Is this the right place?
B: Yes, you've come to the right office. Which language are you interested in?
A: I'd like to study Spanish. I'm a complete beginner.
B: Perfect. We have a beginner Spanish course starting next Monday. It runs every Tuesday and Thursday evening from six to eight.
A: That sounds good. How long is the course?
B: It's a twelve-week course. At the end, you'll take a short assessment to receive your certificate.
A: What materials do I need?
B: We provide the main textbook, but you'll need a notebook and a dictionary. You can buy a recommended dictionary at the campus bookshop.
A: How much does the course cost?
B: The fee is two hundred and forty pounds for the full course. You can pay in two installments if you prefer.
A: That's helpful. Can I register online?
B: You can start the registration online, but you'll need to come in person to show your ID and pay the first installment.
