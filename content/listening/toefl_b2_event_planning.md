A: Hi, I'm calling about the international culture festival next month. I'm the student coordinator. Is this the events office?
B: Yes, it is. How can I help you?
A: We're planning a two-day event in the main quad. I need to check what equipment the university can provide.
B: We can provide tables, chairs, a PA system, and a portable stage. How many tables do you think you'll need?
A: Probably around thirty tables and sixty chairs. We're expecting about twenty student organizations to participate.
B: That's fine. We have plenty. What about the PA system — do you need a microphone and speakers for the whole quad?
A: Yes, we'll have performances and announcements throughout both days. We'll also need a projector and screen for the evening film screening.
B: We can arrange that. You'll need to submit a formal equipment request form at least two weeks before the event. The festival is on the fifteenth and sixteenth, right?
A: That's correct. So I need to submit the form by the first at the latest.
B: Exactly. Also, for outdoor events, you'll need approval from the campus safety office. Have you contacted them?
A: Not yet. I'll do that today. Is there anything else I should know?
B: Make sure you have liability insurance for the event. Student organizations can get a one-day policy through the student union office.
