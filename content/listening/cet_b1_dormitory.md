A: Hey, have you settled into the dorm yet?
B: Pretty much. My roommate is really nice, but the room is smaller than I expected.
A: I know what you mean. Mine too. Did you figure out the hot water schedule?
B: Yes, it's available from six to eight in the morning and seven to ten at night. Miss those windows and you're out of luck.
A: That's tough. What about the laundry room?
B: It's on the first floor. The machines take a campus card, not coins. You need to load money onto it at the service center.
A: Good to know. Is the cafeteria food decent?
B: Window three has the best food — they do a great rice dish with braised pork. Window one is faster if you're in a hurry though.
A: I'll keep that in mind. Are there any rules about guests?
B: Guests of the same gender can visit until ten at night. You have to register them at the front desk.
