A: How was your internship at the tech company this summer?
B: It was really eye-opening. I worked on a data analysis team and got to use Python for real projects.
A: That sounds great. Was it very different from what you learned in class?
B: Definitely. In class everything is clean and structured. At work, the data was messy and incomplete. I spent the first two weeks just cleaning data.
A: Did you feel prepared enough?
B: Mostly. My statistics background helped a lot. But I wish I had practiced more with pandas and visualization tools before starting.
A: Did you get any feedback from your supervisor?
B: Yes, she said my technical skills were solid but I needed to improve my presentation skills. I had trouble explaining technical results to non-technical colleagues.
A: That's a common challenge. Are you going back next year?
B: They offered me a part-time position during the school year, which I accepted. It pays well and I can work remotely two days a week.
