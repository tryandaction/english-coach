A: That's good to hear. Can you describe a challenging project you've worked on and how you handled it?
B: Certainly. Last year, I led a team to migrate our company's entire database to a cloud-based system. The main challenge was minimizing downtime. We planned the migration in phases over six weekends and communicated constantly with all departments.
A: Impressive. How do you handle tight deadlines and pressure?
B: I prioritize tasks by urgency and impact, and I'm not afraid to ask for help when needed. I also find that clear communication with the team prevents most deadline crises.
A: What salary range are you expecting?
B: Based on my research and experience, I'm looking at somewhere between forty-five and fifty-five thousand pounds annually, but I'm open to discussion.
