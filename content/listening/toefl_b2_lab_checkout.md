A: Hi, I'm in Professor Mason's biology course, and I need to borrow a microscope kit for tomorrow's lab.
B: Sure. Do you already have a lab equipment account with us?
A: Yes, I opened one at the beginning of the semester.
B: Great. Then I just need your student ID. The standard kit includes the microscope, two prepared slides, a lens-cleaning cloth, and a storage case.
A: Do I need to return it right after class?
B: Not immediately. Kits borrowed on weekdays are due by five o'clock the following day.
A: That's helpful. I also heard there's a small deposit.
B: Right, it's fifteen dollars, but you get it back when the kit is returned in good condition.
A: Can I pay by card?
B: Yes. We accept card or campus account payment. One more thing: if you need extra slides, those are in Room 204, not here at the front desk.
A: Good to know. I'll just take the standard kit today.
